#### attached after verb
out: from inside to outside
- feel out, turn out, figure out
- read out, speak out, shout out
- point out, multify out, wipe out
- jump out that window
- cut out a wedge


#### combination with of
it means not in

1. get the rig out of the road
2. get out of here
