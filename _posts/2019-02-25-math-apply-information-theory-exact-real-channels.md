#### Gaussian Channel
1. The Gaussian channel has a real input x and a real output y. THe conditional
   distribution of y given x is a Gaussian distribution.
   $$
   P(y | x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \sigma^{2}}} \exp \left[-(y-x)^{2} / 2
   \sigma^{2}\right]
   $$
