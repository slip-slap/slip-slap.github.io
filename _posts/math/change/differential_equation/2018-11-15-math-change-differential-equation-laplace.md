$
F(s)=\int_{0}^{\infty} f(t) e^{-s t} dt
$


1. $I(1) = \frac{1}{s}$
2. $I(t^n) = \frac{n!}{s^{n+1}}$ 
3. $I(sin(at)) = \frac{a}{s^2 - a^2}$
3. $I(cos(at)) = \frac{s}{s^2 + a^2}$
