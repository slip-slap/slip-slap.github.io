#### Example
$$
\int_{x} N\left(x ; \mu_{1}, \sigma_{1}^{2}\right) N\left(x ; y,
\sigma_{2}^{2}\right) d x=N\left(y ; \mu_{1},
\sigma_{1}^{2}+\sigma_{2}^{2}\right)
$$

The key to prove this equation above is $$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^{2}} d x=\sqrt{\pi}
$$.
