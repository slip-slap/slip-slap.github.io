#### Gamma Function
$$
\Gamma(z)=\int_{0}^{\infty} x^{z-1} e^{-x} d x, \quad \mathfrak{R}(z)>0
$$


#### Gauss Integral 
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^{2}} d x=\sqrt{\pi}
$$

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-a(x+b)^{2}} d x=\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{a}}
$$
