#### Definition
One of the most important subgroups of $S_n$ is the set of all even
permutations, $A_n$. The group $A_n$ is called the $\textbf{alternating group on}$ 
n $\textbf{letters}$.


#### Theorem
1. The set $A_n$ is a subgroup of $S_n$
