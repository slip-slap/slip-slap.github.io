#### 堪(たま)らない
1. to express one's uncontrollable emotion
- 会いたくて堪らない: dying to see someone
- 試験の結果が知りたくて堪らない: dying to know the exam result

2. to describe some extreme situation or condition
- 寒くて堪らない: extremely cold
