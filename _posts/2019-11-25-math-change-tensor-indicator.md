#### How to denote a map
$$
\begin{array}{l}
{\Lambda: v \rightarrow w} \\
{\Lambda v \rightarrow w} \\
{\Lambda (v) \rightarrow w} \\
{\langle\Lambda, v\rangle \rightarrow w} \\
{\langle,\Lambda,\rangle} \\
\end{array}
$$

<br />
$$
\langle\Lambda, q v+b p\rangle= q\langle\Lambda, v\rangle+ b\langle\Lambda,
p\rangle
$$
